## Question 1

$$
\begin{aligned}

 F_0(T) &= S_0 e^{(R-q)T} \\ 
R = \frac{1}{T}\text{log}\frac{F_0(T)}{S_0} &= 0.032527
\end{aligned}
$$

implied risk free rate is 3.2527%
